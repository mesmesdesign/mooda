About us

We experience light,
we study light,
and play with light.

MOODA is a lighting research collective operating across three continents. We bring together architects, researchers, and lighting designers who share a singular obsession — the way light shapes human experience, space, and emotion.

Our practice sits at the intersection of technical precision and poetic curiosity. We don't just design lighting systems; we investigate the phenomenology of light, document our findings, and share them openly through our journal.

Each studio brings its own cultural lens. Together, we form a living research network — exchanging ideas across time zones, publishing collaboratively, and occasionally making each other's cities a little brighter.
